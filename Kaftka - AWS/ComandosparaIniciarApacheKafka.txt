Iniciar Zookeeper

.\bin\windows\kafka-server-start.bat .\config\server.properties

Iniciar Apache Kafka

.\bin\windows\kafka-server-start.bat .\config\server.properties


Listar Topicos

.\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --list --zookeeper localhost:2181

Crear Topico

.\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --create --zookeeper localhost:2181 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1 --topic topico-semillero-iot

Crear productor

.\bin\windows\kafka-console-producer.bat --broker-list localhost:9092 --topic topico-semillero-iot

Crear Consumidor

.\bin\windows\kafka-console-consumer.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic topico-semillero-iot --from-beginning

Detener Apache Kafka
.\bin\windows\kafka-server-stop.bat

Detenet Zookeeper

.\bin\windows\zookeeper-server-stop.bat
